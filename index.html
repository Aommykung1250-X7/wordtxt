Introduction

Hello everyone. Today, I‚Äôm going to talk about one of the most serious environmental problems in Thailand and many countries around the world ‚Äî the PM2.5 dust problem. It affects both our health and our daily life quality.

Environmental Problem

PM2.5 refers to very tiny particles ‚Äî smaller than 2.5 microns ‚Äî that can easily enter our lungs and bloodstream. This problem usually gets worse during the cold season, especially in big cities like Bangkok or Chiang Mai.

Causes

The main causes of PM2.5 pollution are:
  1. Open burning, such as burning waste or agricultural areas,
  2. Vehicle emissions, especially in big cities,
  3. Industrial pollution, and weak law enforcement in some areas.

Effects

PM2.5 dust has serious health effects. It can cause respiratory diseases, asthma, allergies, and even heart problems. It also affects the environment by reducing visibility and damaging tourism and the economy.

Solutions
